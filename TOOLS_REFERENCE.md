# DreamConsole Full Tools Reference

This document serves as a complete reference for all **54+ tools** available on the DreamConsole platform. It encompasses both frontend-only utilities and tools backed by the backend API.

## 1. Image Tools
*   **AI Background Remover**: Instantly erase backgrounds from any image using advanced AI. *(Backend)*
*   **JPG to PNG**: Convert JPG images to high-quality PNG format. *(Backend)*
*   **PNG to JPG**: Convert PNG images to standard JPG format. *(Backend)*
*   **WEBP to JPG**: Convert WebP images to widely supported JPG format. *(Backend)*
*   **WEBP to PNG**: Convert WebP images to high-quality PNG format. *(Backend)*
*   **HEIC to JPG**: Convert iPhone HEIC photos to widely supported JPG format. *(Backend)*
*   **HEIC to PNG**: Convert HEIC images to high-quality PNG format. *(Backend)*
*   **Image Compressor**: Compress images to reduce file size without losing quality. *(Backend)*
*   **Resize Image**: Change image dimensions with optional aspect ratio lock. *(Backend)*
*   **Crop Image**: Trim images to an exact region using pixel coordinates. *(Backend)*
*   **Add Watermark**: Protect images with a customizable text watermark. *(Backend)*
*   **Rotate & Flip**: Rotate 90°, 180°, 270° or flip images horizontally and vertically. *(Backend)*
*   **Image to PDF**: Convert a single image to a PDF file. *(Backend)*
*   **Blur & Redact Region**: Blur or black out a region to hide faces or sensitive details. *(Backend)*

## 2. PDF Tools
*   **Merge PDF**: Combine multiple PDF files into one single document. *(Backend)*
*   **Compress PDF**: Reduce the file size of your PDF documents. *(Backend)*
*   **Split PDF**: Extract pages or split every page into separate PDF files. *(Backend)*
*   **PDF to Images**: Convert PDF pages to PNG or JPG images in a ZIP file. *(Backend)*
*   **PDF to Word**: Convert PDF documents to editable Word (.docx) files. *(Backend)*
*   **Word to PDF**: Convert Word (.docx) documents to PDF format. *(Backend)*
*   **PDF Watermark**: Add a text watermark to every page of your PDF. *(Backend)*
*   **Rotate PDF**: Rotate all pages in a PDF by 90°, 180°, or 270°. *(Backend)*
*   **Add Page Numbers**: Stamp page numbers on every page of your PDF document. *(Backend)*
*   **PDF to Text**: Extract plain text from PDF documents. *(Backend)*
*   **Reorder PDF Pages**: Drag and drop page thumbnails to rearrange your PDF. *(Backend)*
*   **Sign PDF**: Add your signature image to a PDF document. *(Backend)*
*   **Images to PDF**: Combine multiple images into one PDF document. *(Backend)*
*   **Protect PDF**: Encrypts a PDF with a user-provided password. *(Backend Route Available)*
*   **Unlock PDF**: Decrypts a password-protected PDF. *(Backend Route Available)*
*   **Extract Metadata**: Retrieves author, creation date, software, and other PDF metadata. *(Backend Route Available)*
